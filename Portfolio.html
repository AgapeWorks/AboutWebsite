began my career as an undergraduate at the Yale University Press, working in book jacket & key art design. Early work as the assistant to Jerelle Kraus, former Art Editor of The New York Times, and studying under John Sabel, former EVP of Creative Print at Disney, instilled in me a love for iconic visual storytelling.

I now lead the creative team at TALISMAN, where I consult and design for Fortune 500 brands, and create identities for budding entrepreneurs; I develop story and character for award-winning series and films; I design identity and sustainable packaging; I use design, words, music, space, people, and time to weave a great story.

On pages, stages, or screens, it's about story, integrity, and forward-thinking collaboration.
